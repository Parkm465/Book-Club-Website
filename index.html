Story Summit Marathon

From Thought to Plot!

About the Event

Our Story Summit Club provides a supportive environment where University of Maryland, College Park students can develop their creative writing skills and bring their unique ideas to life.

Meet weekly for guided workshops that cover essential storytelling elements including character development, plot structure, and world-building techniques.

Session combines instruction with hands-on writing exercises, culminating in opportunities for students to share their work and receive constructive feedback from peers and mentors. This club is designed for college students who have a passion for storytelling, regardless of their current writing experience or skill level.


What to Expect

Unleash Your Voice, Ignite Your Imagination.

  • Networking & Collaboration: Connect with other creatives.
  • Exclusive Resources & Tools : Access guides, feedback sessions, and publishing tips.
  • Inspiring Readings & Contests: Showcase your work and celebrate storytelling.


Event Schedule

Monday - Guided Workshop

Students will explore techniques to transform their personal experiences into compelling fictional narratives.

Wednesday - Peer Review

Students will share their work and receive constructive feedback.

Friday - Final Submission

Students will present their polished short stories or opening chapters.
